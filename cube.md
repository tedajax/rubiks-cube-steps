Yellow Cross
------------

Bar:
    F, R, U, R', U', F'

Hook:
    F, U, R, U', R', F'

Yellow Face
-----------

Fish tail:
    R, U, R', U, R, U2, R'

Yellow Corners
--------------

If no matching corners do pattern twice.
Matching corners on matching face at back:
    R', F, R', B2, R, F', R', B2, R2


Final Rotations
---------------

Clockwise:
    R2, U, R, U, R', U', R', U', R', U, R'

Counterclockwise:
    R, U', R, U, R, U, R, U', R', U', R2
